Intro

I'm a nontraditional student finishing a B.S. in Informatics with a concentration in User Experience at the University at Albany, while working full time in the nonprofit sector. My coursework and my career keep circling the same question: how do we build data systems and technology that actually serve communities, not just function?

My work lives at the intersection of data, technology, and social good. I care about systems that are accountable and equitable, and in practice that looks like telling stories with data, advocating for thoughtful tech adoption in nonprofit and civic spaces, and helping people connect with the resources they need.

If you're working where community impact meets technology, I'd love to connect.
